Privacy Policy · Last updated March 2026

Split is operated by Flavio Deoliveira LLC. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We use your name as your in-game username and your email to identify your account and record purchases.

Game data. We store your scores, game session counts, and the level you played on. This is used to power the leaderboard and track your progress.

Purchase information. If you purchase a Split Pass or Origin Pass, Stripe processes your payment. We receive confirmation of the purchase including the email address used. We do not store your credit card number.

Usage data. We record page visits with a random session ID and the referring URL. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the game, display your name on the leaderboard, verify your purchase across devices and sessions, and provide customer support if you contact us.

We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not use your information for advertising.

Google Sign In

We use Google Sign In to verify your identity. When you sign in, Google shares your name, email address, and profile picture with us. We only use your name and email. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.

Data Storage

Your account and game data is stored securely in Supabase, a hosted database service. Payment records are stored in Stripe. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. We will delete your data within 30 days.

You may sign out of the game at any time using the account menu. Signing out removes your session from the device but does not delete your account or purchase history.

Children

Split is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. We will update the date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@split.games.
